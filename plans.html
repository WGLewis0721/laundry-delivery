How overage works

Every bag has a weight cap set by your plan. When we weigh your bag at pickup, one of two things happens:

  • Within cap — no extra charge. Your subscription covers it.
  • Over cap — we text you the exact overage in pounds and dollars. You see the number before we process anything. The overage is charged to your card on file at delivery, not before.

Overage rate: $2.50 per lb over your bag's weight cap.
