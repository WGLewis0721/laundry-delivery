Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · This is a pre-launch placeholder. Final policy will be reviewed by legal counsel before launch.

Information we collect

When you use FOLD, we may collect: your name, email address, mobile phone number, pickup address, and payment information (processed via Square — we do not store full card numbers).

How we use it

We use your information to schedule and deliver your laundry, send order updates by SMS, process payments, and improve our service. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

SMS communications

By providing your mobile number at signup you consent to receive transactional SMS messages about your order (pickup reminder, weigh-in receipt, delivery date, delivery confirmation). You can reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS notifications.

Payment data

Payment processing is handled by Square. Card data is tokenised and stored by Square; we store only a reference token. Your card is charged only for your subscription and for overage amounts disclosed to you by text before the charge.

Cookies & analytics

We may use essential cookies for session management and analytics to understand how visitors use the site. No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are used.

Data retention

We retain order and account data for as long as your account is active, plus 12 months after cancellation for tax and dispute purposes. You may request deletion by emailing hello@foldlaundry.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@foldlaundry.com
