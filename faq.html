FAQ

Everything you need to know before your first pickup.

We confirm your delivery date after weighing your bags at pickup — typically 1 to 2 business days. You'll get a text the moment it's set. We don't promise a delivery time at booking because we'd sometimes have to break it; instead we give you a real date once we know this week's load.

No surprises. If a bag is over its cap, we text you a weigh-in receipt showing the exact weight, your cap, the overage in pounds, and the dollar amount. We only charge your card on file after you've seen that receipt. Overage rate is $2.50 per lb.

Leave your bags in a safe spot — by the front door, in a lobby, at a gate, or anywhere you're comfortable. Add instructions when you schedule ("front porch, left of the door"). We'll text you when we pick up.

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account dashboard whenever you like. Your access runs through the end of the current billing period.

Anything marked "delicate" or "hang dry" is separated and handled with extra care. Add a note when scheduling ("please hang-dry the blouses") and we'll follow it. For very special items, a professional dry-cleaner is recommended.

We mail your FOLD bags after you subscribe — typically arriving within 3–5 business days. They come back with your clean laundry after every pickup, ready to fill again.

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from your account dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

We pick up within your 2-hour window, so bags are unattended for a short time at most. Choose a spot you're comfortable with — a covered porch, a secure lobby, or a gate. Most customers leave bags right by their door without issue.
