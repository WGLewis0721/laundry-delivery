Our story

We built what we wished existed.

FOLD started with a simple frustration: laundry services that promised a turnaround and then couldn't keep it. We thought — what if instead of overpromising, we just told the truth? Weigh the bags, set a delivery date we can actually meet, and send a text when it's done.

What we believe

  • —
    Honesty over promises. We weigh your bags in front of the record. You pay for what's there, nothing more.
  • —
    Your time is your most valuable possession. We're here to give it back.
  • —
    Reliability is earned, not promised. We'd rather confirm a date we can keep than give you an optimistic one we can't.
  • —
    Service should feel effortless. The best interaction is the one you barely notice.

"We built FOLD because we kept seeing services lead with a turnaround promise they couldn't always keep. We decided transparency was the better play."

— The FOLD team, Montgomery, AL
