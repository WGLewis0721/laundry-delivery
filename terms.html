Service description

FOLD provides subscription laundry pickup and delivery in the Montgomery, AL area. Services include wash & fold, hang-dry care, and delicates handling.

Subscription and billing

Subscriptions are billed monthly. You choose a plan (Solo, Household, or Estate) at signup. Your plan includes a set number of bags per pickup and a per-bag weight cap. Bags that exceed their weight cap are charged at the overage rate ($2.50/lb) against the card on file.

Overage charges

We weigh each bag at pickup. If a bag exceeds its weight cap, we will send you a text message showing the exact weight, cap, overage amount, and dollar charge before your card is charged. By providing a card on file you authorise these overage charges.

Pickup and delivery

You schedule a 2-hour pickup window. We collect your bags during that window. Delivery dates are set after weighing (capacity-driven, not fixed at booking) and communicated to you by SMS. We do not guarantee a specific delivery date at the time of booking.

Contactless pickup

You may leave bags in a safe spot (front door, lobby, gate). FOLD is not responsible for loss or theft of items left unattended. We are not responsible for damage to items not disclosed as delicate at booking.

Cancellation

Cancel anytime from your account dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. No refunds for partial billing periods.

Limitation of liability

To the extent permitted by law, FOLD's liability for any lost or damaged item is limited to the cost of that item up to $50 per bag. We are not liable for dry-clean-only or undisclosed special-care items.

Changes to these terms

We may update these terms. We will notify you by email at least 14 days before material changes take effect.
