Why FOLD

The FOLD difference

We don't make promises we can't keep. We make the ones we can.

⚖️

Weighed by the bag

Every bag is weighed in front of the record at pickup. Your receipt shows the exact weight, the cap, and any overage — nothing is rounded up quietly.

💳

Card on file, receipt first

Overage is charged to your card on file — but only after you've seen the receipt. We text you the math before we process a penny.

📱

Texts, not guesswork

You get an SMS at every step: pickup confirmed, bags weighed, delivery date set, out for delivery, delivered. You always know exactly where your laundry is.

📅

Capacity-honest delivery

We don't promise a delivery time at booking — because we'd sometimes break it. Instead, we set your date once we know this week's load. Honest beats optimistic.

Frequently asked questions

We confirm your delivery date after weighing your bags at pickup — typically 1 to 2 business days. You'll get a text the moment it's set. We don't promise a delivery time at booking because we'd sometimes have to break it; instead we give you a real date once we know this week's load.

No surprises. If a bag is over its cap, we text you a weigh-in receipt that shows the exact weight, your cap, the overage in pounds, and the dollar amount. We only charge your card on file after you've seen that. Overage is $2.50 per lb.

Leave your bags in a safe spot — by the front door, in a lobby, at a gate, or anywhere you'd feel comfortable. Add instructions when you schedule ("front porch, left of the door"). We'll text you when we pick up.

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account dashboard whenever you like. Your access runs through the end of the billing period.
