Services

Everything we offer, explained plainly.

🧺

Wash & Fold

Standard laundry washed in cold water, tumble dried at medium heat, and neatly folded. Your everyday load.

👗

Hang-Dry Care

Items you mark "hang dry" are separated from the main wash, air-dried on hangers, and returned on a hanger inside the bag.

✨

Delicates Handling

Delicate items are washed in a mesh bag on a gentle cycle, never high heat. Available on Household and Estate plans.

What we don't handle: Dry-clean-only garments, leather, suede, and items requiring professional pressing. For these we recommend a local specialist.
