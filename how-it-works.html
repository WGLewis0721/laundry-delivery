How FOLD works

From your front door to clean folded clothes and back — without a trip to the laundromat. Three steps. Zero hassle.

Step 1

Pick a plan & get your bags

Choose the subscription tier that fits your household — Solo, Household, or Estate. We mail you your FOLD bags (one per scheduled pickup) within a few days.

Each bag has a weight cap built into your plan. Everything is weighed at pickup — you'll see the exact number before you're charged anything.

Step 2

Schedule a pickup

Pick a 2-hour window online — up to seven days ahead. We work around your schedule, not the other way around.

Leave your filled FOLD bag by the door, in a lobby, or at a gate. Add a note for any special instructions ("front porch, left of door · gate code #1234"). Contactless pickup — no need to be home.

Schedule a pickup
Step 3

We weigh, wash & deliver

Every bag is weighed on a certified scale at pickup. You get a text with the exact weight, your cap, and any overage — nothing is rounded up quietly.

We wash and fold everything according to your care instructions, then set a delivery date based on this week's capacity. You'll get a text when it's confirmed, and another when it's at your door.
